Welcome! We believe "Health is Wealth" and are committed to serving the community.

About Us

We are dedicated to providing high-quality healthcare services to the underserved communities of Gadchiroli. Our mission is to bring healing and medical advancements closer to those in need.

Our Facilities

With a 30-bed capacity, modern diagnostic services, and expert medical staff, we offer comprehensive healthcare solutions, including emergency care, maternity services, general medicine, and pediatrics.

Community Health Initiatives

Our outreach programs focus on preventive healthcare, awareness campaigns, and free medical camps to ensure that no one is left behind in receiving medical attention.

Blood Donation Drive

We regularly organize blood donation camps in collaboration with government agencies to save lives and contribute to a healthier society.
